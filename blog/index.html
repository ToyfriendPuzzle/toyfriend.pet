What's it been up to, huh?

Yeah I've been lazing about updating the site... but that just means I have more to share!

Uh... I guess a whole lot and a lotta little has happened so far this year? I got into some new artistic mediums. Scanography, sculpture(?), and painting. (I'll start putting galleries on this site... EVENTUALLY!).

My Nikon Df came back from the shop after months still broken, dealing with having no Good camera (no offense to my digicams...) from December to May sucked a lot. Getting into the above mentioned mediums in that time was a silver lining. I picked up a Pentax APS-C DSLR to replace it (on the off-chance that the Df can be resurrected it'll be something different). The new camera is pretty snazzy, but I do find myself missing good ol manual focus on a through a good focusing screen.

If you're interested in seeing my photo experiments and/or do photography yourself, please check out the daily photo club!

Oh, yeah, also my friends and I started a newspaper! We need submissions! And readers! Go to bigolnews.org to learn more!

I've also been jamming and performing music a lot more. Yet another thing I need to throw onto this site. Bwahhhh. It'll happen. It'll happen.

I think that's it for this update. I'm not gonna say anything like "I hope the next update doesn't take as long." because I could see that getting really funny if the next update is like near the end of the year. So I will just say: until next time! May your life be weird!

20250101

MR. BEAST DESTROYS BELOVED CAMERA

I wish this was a joke, I wish that my beloved and beat-up Nikon Df had not died under the soul-less gaze of Mr. Beast's half-smiling face1, yet here we are...

The above photograph is the final one taken by my beloved photosmachine. It has not once shown a hickup in its function, has never had any issues, any weirdness, nothin'. I'm walking around, I see a Mr. Beast cutout, I tell my creature to pose with it, I take the shot. Eh, not enough dread, recompose the shot, press the shutter release again, then- BAM- -Err- displayed on the screen. Mirror slaps on pressing the shutter release, but the shutter does not open. The camera is currently sitting in a local camera repair shop with an uncertain fate. It may or may not be fixable. If it's not fixable, I'm fucked. I cannot afford a new one. Mr. Beast, if you're out there somewhere, please buy me a new camera.

1 Mr. Beast only smiles with his mouth. If you cover that billion-dollar smile, you can clearly see an emptiness in those million-dollar eyes.
